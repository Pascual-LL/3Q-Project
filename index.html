⋆ Gracie Abrams ⋆

Gracie Madigan Abrams is a singer-songwriter, who creates calm, indie-pop songs. Her music gives us a cozy, calm mood, with her soft vocals and unique sounds incorporated into the songs.


⋆ Career ⋆


Born on September 7, 1999, she grew up in Los Angeles County, California. At a young age, she played different musical instruments such as the drums, piano, guitar and ukulele.

Eventually, she gained a following on social media for her earlier songs. She expanded her music career, releasing her first single, "Mean It", in 2019.

She was an opening act for Olivia Rodrigo in 2022, and later an opening act for Taylor Swift in 2023, even singing a duet with her.

She's grown not just as a singer, but as an artist, with 2 EPs, and 1 album, all successful. She was also nominated for "best new artist" at the 2024 Grammy Awards. She continues to grow in her career, putting much work and skill in her music.
