⋆ Trivia ⋆


"Audrey" is a name often mentioned in Gracie's songs. Audrey is her best friend, and her song "Feels Like" is about the time she and Audrey took a train from New York to Conneticut to watch a movie.

If Swifties have friendship bracelets, Gracelanders have bows. It is impossible to not spot a ribbon when attending one of Gracie's concerts. It's like an unspoken dress code for her fans.

Gracie is a lover of poetry and writing. She has been journaling for a long time, and draws inspiration from her own journal after writing down her thourghts.
