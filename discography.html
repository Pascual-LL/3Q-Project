Minor, Gracie's first EP released in 2020, contains soft vocals and lyrics about tough relationships and friendships from the past. Its songs contain unique, brain-scratching sounds, with gentle harmonies.

Gracie's first album, released in 2023, contains a mix of calm songs and others with more somber moods. Lyrically, she experiments with sharp changes in her bridges, which still complement the mood of her songs. Love, friendship and loss is tackled.
